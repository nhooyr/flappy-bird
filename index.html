Minimal HTML, CSS and JavaScript demo game based on:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flappy_Bird

You are the yellow square. Your goal is to jump through the pipes. You die if you touch a pipe or fall to the ground.

You gain a point every pipe you pass.

The game gets harder as you gain more points.

The game is publicly hosted at:
https://nhooyr.github.io/flappy-bird

The source code is available at:
https://github.com/nhooyr/flappy-bird

Controls

  1. Click or tap to start.

  2. Click or tap to jump through the pipes.

  3. Click or tap to play again after dying.
